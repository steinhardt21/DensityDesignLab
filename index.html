A country as an economic power

THE GDP AS MARKETCAP
The infographic creates a visual hierarchy since the economical power of the Internet companies is demonstrated by the size of their Area: the richest companies are also the biggest. To quantify the power more deeply, the companies are compared to a real country in the world (Denmark). This country was chosen as the basis of comparison because it is a well-known highly developed European nation, with a GDP theoretically acquirable by the companies. The economic size of Denmark is shown inside of the company to visualize how small it is, compared to the biggest one.

A country as a comunity of individuals

EMPLOYEES AS POPOLATION
Usually, the economical power of a country is made by domestic labor. However, in internet companies the power is not generated by the human presence directly. A lot of rich companies are machine based and they don’t need a massive amount of employees. Every billion of the company value of Facebook was generated by only 37 of its employees

Flags as business category

HOW INTERNET COMPANIES MAKE MONEY
Standard flags of cartographic maps are interpreted into new ones to represent a system of categorization. Through the different layouts is possible to understand directly which is the main business of each company and how they appear to the costumers. Moreover, the colors of the flags were extracted from the identity of the companies they are referring to.
